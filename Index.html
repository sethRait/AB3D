Assassins

The Game:

Assassins (A.K.A. Gotcha! or Paraonia) is a stealth live action game. Each player is given a list of targets that he/she must eliminate via any means necessary (tagging, nerf guns, etc). Players earn points by eliminating the players who populate their kill lists. The person(s) with the most points at the end of each round will have won.
